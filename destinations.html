Sigiriya (Lion Rock): The Eighth Wonder

A majestic rock fortress rising 200 meters from the jungle floor, Sigiriya is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient palace ruins, stunning water gardens, and beautiful, historic frescoes. It offers a breathtaking 360-degree view of the cultural triangle.

Highlights & Activities:

  • Climb the **Lion Paw Staircase** to the summit.
  • View the **Sigiriya Damsels** (frescoes) halfway up.
  • Walk through the symmetrical **Water Gardens**.
  • Visit the nearby **Dambulla Cave Temple** (day trip).
